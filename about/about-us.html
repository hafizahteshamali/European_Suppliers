About Us

CIT Electronics is a leading electronics wholesaler selling thousands of used devices daily.

We are CIT Electronics

CIT Electronics' home is an advanced facility in the tech corridor near Salt Lake City, Utah, USA. Our leadership team possesses over 35 years of combined experience in the mobile device marketplace, and nearly 100 years managing and leading successful enterprises in a host of industries.

Four keys to success

We were founded by an entrepreneur with over a decade of prior experience in creating and growing businesses in the cell phone industry. He continues to lead and manage CIT, focusing us on four keys to success:

Mutual Success

Creating lasting vendor and customer relationships built on shared mutual success.

International Service

Growing our customer base through web marketing methods while expanding our international service

Improvement & Progress

Executing efficient and timely operations while continuously improving processes.

Hiring Talent

Seeking and hiring exceptional new talent into the CIT family.

Company Values

We demonstrate a dedication to our customers through competitive pricing, daily inventory updates, and fast shipping of reliable, quality products.
